Introduction

In 2018, we ran a workshop on Kubernetes in PyConUK. This workshop had exactly one rule: Show me or it didn't happen.

What does this even mean? Well, for instance:

⦿ Instead of telling people about how Kubernetes restart pods that fail, we would literally put code in our application that crashes it, hit that code and observe what happens.

⦿ Instead of telling people about Kubernetes manifests, we will not use them, get into trouble and hopefully show exactly why they exist.

⦿ Instead of using toy applications to get the concepts across, we will try and build a clone of a proper service, fail and noodle around for a solution just like we would in our day jobs.

Its 2020 & the world seems to have gone to sh*t. So we thought let's reproduce this workshop in the form of a book.
